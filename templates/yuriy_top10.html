Yuriy's Top 10

Photo Contest

Entries are judged by aesthetic quality and storytelling. Photos that best meet the criteria below make the Top 10.

Judging Criteria

Aesthetic Criteria (The Professional "Eye")

  • Rule of Thirds & Symmetry — Subjects on power points or perfectly centered for impact.
  • Color Harmony — Complementary palettes and strong vibrancy without looking over-processed.
  • Visual Complexity — Clean shots with a clear subject score high; cluttered or random scenes score low.
  • Horizon Alignment — Tilted horizons are penalized unless it's an intentional Dutch tilt for action.

Contextual "Best" (The Human Factor)

  • Uniqueness / Diversity — Among similar shots (e.g. many sunsets), the one with the most unique light or moment is chosen; the rest are suppressed.
  • Emotional Peak — At events, high-energy moments (big laughs, tears, hugs) are prioritized.

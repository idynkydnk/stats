About TrueSkill

TrueSkill was developed by Microsoft Research for ranking and matchmaking in games. It models each player's skill as a Gaussian distribution and updates these distributions after each game.

The Rating shown is the conservative skill estimate (μ - 3σ), meaning there's a 99% confidence the player's true skill is above this value.
